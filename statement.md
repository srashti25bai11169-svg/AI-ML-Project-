# 📄 Project Statement: Study Time Suggestion AI

## 📌 Problem Statement

Students often struggle to manage their study time effectively, especially when preparing for exams. They may not know how much time to dedicate to different subjects based on difficulty level, available time, and exam deadlines. This can lead to poor time management, stress, and reduced academic performance.

---

## 🎯 Proposed Solution

The **Study Time Suggestion AI** is a Python-based system that provides personalized study time recommendations. It uses a rule-based approach to analyze user inputs such as free time, subject difficulty, and days remaining before exams, and suggests an optimal number of study hours per day.

---

## 🧠 Approach

The system follows a simple Artificial Intelligence technique using **decision rules (heuristics)**:

* Assigns base study time depending on subject difficulty
* Increases study time as exam dates approach
* Adjusts recommendations based on the user’s available free time
* Provides additional study tips for better productivity

---

## ⚙️ Key Inputs

* Free hours available per day
* Subject difficulty (easy, medium, hard)
* Number of days left for the exam

---

## 📤 Output

* Recommended number of study hours per day
* Helpful suggestions for improving study efficiency

---

## 🌟 Expected Outcome

* Better time management for students
* Reduced exam stress
* Improved study planning and productivity

---

## 🚀 Scope for Enhancement

* Multi-subject planning
* Weekly or monthly timetable generation
* Integration with real datasets and machine learning models
* GUI or mobile app implementation

---

## 🗣️ Conclusion

This project demonstrates how basic AI techniques can be applied to solve real-world problems like study planning. It is simple, effective, and can be expanded into a more advanced intelligent system.
